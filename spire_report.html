Understanding the Numbers
Stats Guide
Every stat explained — what it measures, why it matters, and what a high score means.
⚔ Aggression Index — PTH / BLD / REF
PTH
Pathing Aggression. How dangerously you navigate the map. Measures elite density per floor, gold investment ratio, and HP% when choosing to fight elites. High PTH = you fight elites at low HP and spend gold aggressively.
BLD
Build Aggression. How offensively you construct your deck, normalized by character. Measures attack ratio vs character baseline, aggressive power usage. High BLD = attack-heavy, aggressive power choices.
REF
Refinement. Deck cleanliness and discipline. Rewards removing starter cards and curses, penalizes bloat. High REF = lean, purposeful deck. Only stat that directly predicts wins (+0.228 gap).
✦ Skill Line — SKL / CON / GRW
SKL
Skill Rating. How much you outperform the expected win rate at your chosen difficulty. Accounts for ascension level, floor depth, and attempt bonus for pushing harder content. High SKL = winning more than you should at your ascension.
CON
Consistency. Does your deck building quality translate to wins? Measures REF gap between wins and losses, and archetype commitment on winning vs losing runs. High CON = you execute well when it matters.
GRW
Growth. Are you improving over time? Compares early vs late runs on SKL improvement, REF trend, and ascension climbing rate. High GRW = meaningful progression within your play period.
◎ Performance Stats — EXP / CLT / ADP / EFF / RSL
EXP
Experience. Archetype mastery depth. Peak win rate on your best archetype, breadth of archetypes tried, improvement on most-played strategy.
CLT
Clutch. Performance under pressure. Win rate arriving at bosses below 40% HP, win rate after going below 30% HP mid-run.
ADP
Adaptability. Breadth of viable archetypes and characters. How many different strategies and characters you win with.
EFF
Efficiency. Resource conversion quality. Relics extracted per elite fought, upgrades per smith, gold value per shop visit.
RSL
Resilience. Recovery from early adversity. Win rate on runs where you were at low HP early, took heavy act 1 damage, or had a brutal first elite.
★ Archetype Tiers
MASTER
6+ runs, winning at double the expected rate for your ascension level.
EXPERT
5+ runs, outperforming expected win rate by 50%+.
PROFICIENT
Beating expected win rate but not yet at Expert threshold.
PRACTICED
10+ runs but at or below expected win rate. Experience without results yet.
APPRENTICE
3-9 runs, still learning.
NOVICE
Fewer than 3 runs. Insufficient data.
